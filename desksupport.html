I am a salesperson; why am I receiving the message "403 access forbidden" when I try to click on features of the desktop?

Sales people are generally assigned the role of Salesperson (App Only) by their compliance officer or senior staff members upon the creation of their user account.
Everyone is able to login to the AMLHub desktop (amlhub.co.nz) and the AMLHub app (downloaded from either the App Store or the Google Play Store). However, users who have app only access i.e. Salesperson (App Only) and Admin (App Only) will not be able to access any feature of their entity on the desktop due to not being granted any permissions for this access.
Please refer to the AMLHub User Roles & Permissions document for further clarification on each role's level of access within the AMLHub.

What is the breakdown of definitions for each benefical owner type?

You are identifying how the client is a ‘beneficial owner’, where some individuals may meet a couple of these categories:
  1. 25%+ ownership: this is where someone owns more than 25% of the asset, meaning they are a beneficial owner.
  2. Trustee: trustee of the trust. These are the named trustees in a Trust Deed. For example, for a family trust this would typically be the husband and wife, and their lawyer.
  3. Settlor: Settlor of the trust. This is the named Settlor in a Trust Deed
  4. Controller: someone who is in effective control of the asset i.e. Managing Director, who may also be a 25%+ shareholder.
  5. Acting on behalf: someone who is instructing you on behalf of the owner. This maybe a lawyer or power of attorney.
  6. Executor: the executor of the will.

Password Reset

How do I reset my Password if I cannot login to the AMLHub?

If you are unable to login to the app or desktop with your credentials i.e. are entering an incorrect password/have forgotten password, you are able to reset it from the login page:
  1. Visit the AMLHub desktop login page using the following URL https://amlhub.co.nz/login
  2. Click “Forgot Password?”.
  3. Enter the email address associated with your user account.
  4. Confirm you are not a robot (tick the box)
  5. Select “send reset link”.
  6. An email with a link to create a new password will be sent to your email address. Please check your junk mail if you cannot find this email in your primary inbox.

If you would like to change your password and you are able to log in to the AMLHub desktop, please follow the procedure below:

How do i delete a duplicate deal?

Please note: when a sale is made or a client is lost, these deals should be moved to the “completed deals” section of your CDD Register as opposed to being deleted.
Deleting a deal can only be done by someone with access to the CDD Register on the AMLHub desktop. To delete a deal in your CDD Register, the deal must be unverified..
  1. Visit the AMLHub desktop login page using the following URL https://amlhub.co.nz/login
  2. Login using the following credentials:
    3. Username: Email Address Password: as you created it through the verification email, unless you have reset it since then (must be a minimum of 6 characters)
  3. You will be directed to the homepage, where it asks you to select an entity. Please click on the entity of which you would like to proceed with.
  4. Navigate to your CDD Register either by clicking on the CDD Register tile or on the CDD Register section in the left-hand menu bar.

  7. Select "delete".

How do I assign users to a deal through the AMLHub desktop following the creation of a deal?

When you are confident with the information and documentation uploaded to each individual and the deal itself, you may undergo the verification process in order to convert the deal to a valid listing.

You must verify each individual loaded to the deal before you can verify the deal itself. If you attempt to verify the deal prior to doing so, you will be given the message “cannot verify a deal with unverified children”.

We refer to the deal and its individuals/parties as working like a parent-child relationship. You cannot verify the parent until you have verified all of the children.

I am seeing users from Branch A in a drop down menu when I go to assign users from Branch B to a deal in Branch B. How do I find the correct drop
down list for this branch

When assigning users to a property in Branch A, the drop down menu will be consistent with Branch A users. If you then proceed to Branch B to assign users, however, and the drop down menu from Branch A is being remembered instead, please see below.

You can resolve this problem by refreshing your webpage, where the drop down menu will change to be consistent with the branch you are in at the time of refreshing. For example, once you access the CDD Register of Branch A, refreshing the page should subsequently bring up a drop down menu consistent with the Branch A users.

What is the difference between the terms unverified, verified, completed and all deals?

  1. Unverified deals: initial creation of a deal will result in the deal appearing here. Creation of a deal can be done by salespeople through the application or by senior staff through either the application or the desktop.

  2. Verified deals: unverified deals will be moved to this section once the verification process has been completed by someone who has received appropriate training to do so i.e. AMLCO, AMLSO, Owner, Admin Staff. Once verified, deals can become a listing.

  3. Completed deals: deals should be moved to this category from the “verified deals” section if a sale is made or the client is lost i.e. they should not be deleted. The only deals that should be deleted/removed from the CDD Register are duplicates/test entries.

  4. All deals: displays a list of all deals in the CDD Register i.e. unverified, verifies and completed deals.
