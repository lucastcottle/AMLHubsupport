Email Verification

How do I verify my email address?

Upon the creation of a user account in the AMLHub, new users are sent an email to verify their email address and create a password.

Click the "verify email" link found in the verification email sent to you, where from there you can create a password and establish a login for your user account. Following the creation of this login, your status as a user will change from "pending" to "active".

If you cannot find this verification email in your primary inbox, please check your junk mail. Alternatively, someone with access to the users page of the AMLHub website can re-send this verification email to your account.

I have been added as a user to AMLHub but haven't received a verification email.

Upon the creation of a user account in the AMLHub, new users are sent an email to verify their email address and create a password. This email can sometimes be filtered to junk mail, depending on security settings. If you cannot find this verification email in your primary inbox, please check your junk mail. You will not be able to log into the AMLHub until you have created a password from this verification email. Once this has been done, your user status will change from “pending” to “active”.

Downloading the AMLHub App

How do I download the AMLHub app for iPhone

  1. Vist the App Store
  2. Type "amlhub" in the search bar.

  3. Select "get" to begin download.

How do I download the AMLHub app for android?

  1. Vist the Google Play Store.
  2. Type "amlhub" in the search bar.

  3. Select "install" to begin download.

Which Apple devices are compatible with the AMLHub app?

Any iPhone or iPad with IOS11 software and higher is able to download the AMLHub app from the App Store.

You can check your version of iOS in the Settings app under General --> About.

Which Android devices are compatible with AMLHub app?

Any Android device with version 5.0 software and higher is able to download the AMLHub app from the Google Play Store.

You can check your version of Android in the Settings app under System --> About Phone.

I have searched for "amlhub" in either the AppStore or the Google Play Store and it doesn't appear as a result.

Your phone region must be set to New Zealand for the AMLHub app to be displayed in either the App Store or Google Play Store.

If you have purchased your phone or used your mobile device overseas, your region settings may not be set to New Zealand. Please check your language & region settings in either the Google Play Store or App Store to ensure your phone is set to New Zealand.

General

Do I need to be in cellphone coverage to use the app?

Yes, the app requires you be in cellphone coverage (with data or WiFi) when uploading or editing information. Otherwise, your information does not save.


What is the breakdown of definitions of each beneficial owner type?

You are identifying how the client is a ‘beneficial owner’, where some individuals may meet a couple of these categories:
  1. 25% ownership: this is where someone owns more than 25% of the asset, meaning they are a beneficial owner.

  2. Trustee: trustee of the trust. These are the named trustees in a Trust Deed. For example, for a family trust this would typically be the husband and wife, and their lawyer.

  3. Effective Controller: someone who is in effective control of the asset i.e. Managing Director, who may also be a 25%+ shareholder.

  4. Acting on behalf:: someone who is in effective control of the asset i.e. Managing Director, who may also be a 25%+ shareholder.

  5. Executor the executor of the will.
Password

How do I reset my password if I cannot login to the AMLHub?

If you are unable to login to the app or desktop with your credentials i.e. are entering an incorrect password/have forgotten password, you are able to reset it from the login page:

  1. Visit the AMLHub desktop login page using the following URL https://amlhub.co.nz/login
  2. Click “Forgot Password?”.
  3. Enter the email address associated with your user account.
  4. Confirm you are not a robot (tick the box).
  5. Select “send reset link”.
  6. An email with a link to create a new password will be sent to your email address. Please check your junk mail if you cannot find this email in your primary inbox.

How do I change my password if I know my current password and can login to the AMLHub?

If you are unable to login to the app or desktop with your credentials i.e. are entering an incorrect password/have forgotten password, you are able to reset it from the login page:

  1. Visit the AMLHub desktop login page using the following URL https://amlhub.co.nz/login
  2. Login using the following credentials:
    3. Username: Email Address Password: as you created it through the verification email, unless you have reset it since then (must be a minimum of 6 characters)
  3. You will be directed to the homepage. Click on the blue downwards arrow next to your name in the top right-hand corner of the screen
  4. Click “profile”.


  5. You will be taken to the “user details” page (see below). Navigate to “change password” using the menu tabs.


  6. Enter your current password.
  7. Type in your new password and then re-type this in the box below.
  8. Click “save”.

Document Upload

How do I upload documents to a stand alone individual or an individual within a party?

  1. From the "details" page of the deal, navigate to the "owners" section using the menu tabs just underneath the deal name (towards the top of the screen).
  2. Tap the individual or party (which the individual is loaded to) you would like to proceed with.
  3. You will be taken to the details page of either a party (company, trust etc.) or individual. If you are on the details page of a party, navigate to the "owners" section using the menu bar just underneath the deal name and tap on the individual you would like to upload documents to.
  4. From the details page of an individual, tap on the "documents" tab using the menu bar just underneath the deal name.
  5. Once on the documents page, tap "add documents".
  6. Displayed on the “create owner document” page should be the options to take a photograph or browse your device’s library.
    • If taking a photograph: select “take a photo”, capture the image and select “use photo” if you are satisfied with its quality. Press “next” in the bottom right hand corner of the screen.
    • If browsing your library: select “browse” and choose image you would like to use. Press “next” in the bottom right hand corner of the screen.
  7. Once you have tapped "next"
    • Enter name of document.
    • Select document type.
    • Press "save" in the bottom right hand corner of the screen.
    • You will be taken to the “documents” section of the individual you uploaded these documents to.
  8. To upload more documents against this individual, press “add document” and repeat process above.

    9. You are only able to upload one image at a time. For example, you cannot add two photos of the front and back of a driver’s license in the same document.
    These should be uploaded as separate documents and, for example, titled "driver's license front" and "driver's license back".
    Please refer to CDD guidelines for required documents.

Updating Deals

I can't find any deals on the "update a deal" page of my app.

Users will only be able to view deals in their "update deals" section of the app that have been assigned to them by senior staff members through the AMLHub desktop (previously created deals) or by themselves/sales agents through the AMLHub app (upon the creation of a deal). This is for security reasons, to prevent all sales agents from an entity having access to update any property.

Upon the creation of a deal in the app, sales agents are provided with the option to assign staff members to their particular deal.

If they do not do this or following the creation of the deal more users need to be assigned, please refer to the CDD Register section of our desktop support FAQs or our desktop manual for how to assign users through the AMLHub desktop.

Please note: anyone who wants to be able to edit the deal through the app should be assigned to it (can be more than one). All senior staff with access to the CDD Register on the AMLHub desktop are already able to update any property through the desktop without needing to be assigned to it. However, if they would like to edit this property through the app too, they will need to be assigned.

How do I Update details of a deal?

  1. Select "update a deal" from the homepage of the app.
  2. Select the deal you would like to proceed with from the list of those assigned to you.
  3. You will be taken to the details page of that particular deal. Select "edit details".
  4. You will be guided through several pages where you can make changes. Tap the blue "next" button on each page after you are satisfied with your updates.

How do I update details of a party?

  2. Select the deal you would like to proceed with from the list of those assigned to you.
  3. You will be taken to the details page of that particular deal. Navigate to the "owners" section using the menu tabs just underneath the deal name (towards the top of the screen).
  4. Tap the party (company, trust etc.) you would like to proceed with.
  5. You will be taken to the details page of that party. Select "edit details".
  6. You will be guided through several pages where you can make changes. Tap the blue "next" button on each page after you are satisfied with your updates.

How do I update details of a stand alone individual within a party?

  2. Select the deal you would like to proceed with from the list of those assigned to you.
  3. You will be taken to the details page of that particular deal. Navigate to the "owners" section using the menu tabs just underneath the deal name (towards the top of the screen).
  4. Tap the party (company, trust etc.) you would like to proceed with.
  5. You will be taken to the details page of that party. Select "edit details".
  6. You will be guided through several pages where you can make changes. Tap the blue "next" button on each page after you are satisfied with your updates.
